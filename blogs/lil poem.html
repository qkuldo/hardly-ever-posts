bushwol

there’s a bush
with little feet
in my little hometown woodland…

a bit shy
a bit aloof
and he always want some of that good berry juice!

it appeared
in my life
when my world wasn’t feeling very bright
when the world’s weight was more than just some big-ass tree
and crashing down on me.

when i looked at it with my very own eyes
i just couldn’t help but be filled with delight
at the little 'noxius face
i opened up my arms to his leafy lair
but he bumped into me and ran away…

the very next day
i tried again
in my little hometown woodland
i got some berries
very nice
and went up to that very same place
where i saw, when i looked,
when/then i was filled with that familiar delight
and reached out my off’ring to the little guy
and he accepted it with a pretty bright smile

ever since then
i’ve been alright
with my bush in my hometown woodland
he’s very nice
he’ll warm up your life
with the very same look that saved my life

but then again
i’m just a guy
with my world just feeling right
now the world’s weight has gotten numb on me
with that little friend by my side.
